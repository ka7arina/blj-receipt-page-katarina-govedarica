Step 1

Crack two eggs and put them into a bowl. Add 3 Tablespoons of sugar. Add a small pack of vanilla sugar and a pinch of salt. Add ca. 150ml of your Liquid.

This could be water, oat milk, or anything else. I typically use sparkling water. Make sure to add as much as feels right to you; there is not an exact measurement.

Step 2

Take a whisk and start whisking. Carefully add as much flour as needed to remove clumps.

Whisk the dough until it's fluid and doesn't have any clumps anymore and looks light enough.

Step 3

Prepare your pan by heating it well, then add a tiny bit of oil and spread it around the pan with a paper roll.

Make sure not to use too much oil. You don't want the pan to be fully covered in oil; you just want it to have a bit of oil so the crêpes don't stick to the bottom of the pan.
